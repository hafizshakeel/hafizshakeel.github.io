BIO

My research interests lie at the intersection of advanced image processing, computer vision, and machine learning, with a particular focus on developing innovative solutions in these areas. I am deeply engaged in exploring new techniques in image processing and computer vision, advancing machine learning models and computational photography, and applying deep learning to tasks such as image manipulation, restoration, segmentation, inpainting, super-resolution, low-light image enhancement, deblurring, denoising, dehazing, detection, tracking, and underwater image processing.

My work also encompasses video processing and compression, 3D vision and depth perception, and the development and application of generative AI and transformer networks. I am particularly interested in integrating vision and language models within multimodal AI systems. My aim is to push the boundaries of these fields and make impactful contributions through cutting-edge research and practical applications.

In addition to my research, I actively explore and master AI/deep learning models including CNNs, GANs, Transformers, etc., harnessing their potential to achieve my research objectives. I also implement AI models to solve vision and NLP-related problems and upload simplified code on my GitHub repository. Check that out!

I am currently seeking research fellowship opportunities and am eager to collaborate with others who are passionate about AI in computer vision. Let's connect and discuss how we can work together to transform and solve problems in this exciting field.
